Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-01-01

Japanese Pick Today (“the App”) is developed and operated by Seunghoon Kam (“we”, “us”, or “our”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

We do NOT collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, or files
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Any personally identifiable information

No login, account creation, or registration is required.

2. Data Used by the App

(1) Word of the Day & Local Content

The App displays daily Japanese words, definitions, and examples. All dictionary/word data is stored locally within the App bundle. No data is sent to any external server.

(2) Favorites / Local Saved Data

If you save words or settings, this data is stored only on your device using Apple’s local storage system (UserDefaults). We cannot access or retrieve this data.

(3) Push Notifications (Optional)

If you enable notifications, the App may send daily reminders. Notification scheduling is processed locally on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

(4) Advertising (AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner ads. Google may collect device identifiers, ad performance data, and approximate location. This data is processed only by Google and is not accessible to us.

For more information, please review Google’s policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Policy Overview

(5) In-App Purchase: Remove Ads

The App offers an optional one-time purchase to remove ads. Purchases are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. No other third-party services collect or process your data. We do not share any information with third parties beyond what is processed by Google.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not collect personal information from children.

5. Data Security

Since no personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted, there is no risk of external data exposure. All settings and saved words remain on your device.

6. Your Rights

Because the App does not collect personal information, users do not need to request deletion, modification, or export of personal data. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this document.
